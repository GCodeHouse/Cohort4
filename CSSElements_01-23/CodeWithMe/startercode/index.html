Katherine Johnson

The human computer and hidden figure

Katherine Johnson, part of small group of African-American women mathematicans who did crucial work at NASA

Here's a timeline of Katherine Johnson's life:

  • 1928 - Katherine finishes eight grade at the age of 10.
  • 1936 - Katherine graduates college at the age of 18.
  • 1958 - Katherine gets assigned the first man space mission.

"Girls are capable of doing everything men are capable of doing. Sometimes they have more imagination than men."

--Katherine Johnson
